# Integrations

## ADP

Today, payouts approved in Peerspectiv are re-keyed by hand into ADP for
1099 reporting and tax handling. This is the dominant manual workflow
left in the payout pipeline.

**Goal:** eliminate the re-keying step. After a payout is approved (and
optionally after the Aautipay external payout settles), push the
matching record into ADP so 1099 totals stay in sync.
